TRANSLATED THROUGH FLAME (5 ESSENCE) 
Your ally disappears, then reappears in a burst of fire.
Fire, Magic, Ranged, Void Main action
Distance: Ranged 10 Target: Self or one ally
Effect: The target is teleported to another space within 
distance. Make a power roll that affects each enemy 
adjacent to the target’s new space.
Power Roll + Reason:
• 11 or lower: 3 fire damage
• 12–16: 5 fire damage
• 17+: 8 fire damage
