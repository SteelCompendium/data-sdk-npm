INTERCEDE (9 W RATH)
You take your ally’s place.
Magic, Ranged Free triggered
Distance: Ranged 10 Target: One ally
Trigger: A creature makes a strike against the target.
Effect: The target is unaffected by the strike and you
become the target instead, even if you aren’t a valid target
for it. You take half the damage from the strike, and the
target gains 3 surges.
